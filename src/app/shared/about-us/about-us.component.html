What We Do

"Joy of Age" is a digital platform for elders to engage within a trusted community of elders and caregivers. Supported by Tata Trust, the pilot launch in 2020 for Hyderabad and the platform intends to reach across to elders in urban and rural India soon.

Our Community

"Joy of Age" will be an online community for members to share their stories & conversations based on their real life experiences. Members can easily participate and drive the content of the conversations based on their relevance and interests.

Ask Experts

"Joy of Age" partners with well-known experts across various functions relevant to the community. Members can ask questions and receive answers in complete confidentiality from these experts.

Products that help

"Joy of Age" presents curated set of products that will be useful to the members. Members can also rate and review products on this platform after using them, to help others make better choices. Purchase and product delivery will be through reliable external partner sites

Services that matter

"Joy of Age" makes it easy for its members to find relevant service providers. Transparent, updated information about the source of data and easy access to reviews from community members will assist elders and their caregivers towards making informed choices before engaging a service provider.
