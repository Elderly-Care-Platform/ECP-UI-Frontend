What We Do

"Joy of Age" supported by Tata Trust, is a digital platform for elders to engage within a trusted community of elders and caregivers. With a pilot launch in late 2019 for Hyderabad, the platform intends to cover larger number of elders in urban and rural India shortly after.

Our Community

"Joy of Age" enables an online community where members share stories and conversations based on real life experiences. Community members can easily participate and drive the content of the conversations based on relevance and interests.

Ask Experts

"Joy of Age" partners with well known experts across various areas relevant to the community. Members can ask questions and receive answers in complete confidentiality from these experts.

Products that help

"Joy of Age" presents curated set of products that are useful to the community. Community members who have used these products can rate and review products on this platform to help other members make better choices. Ability to purchase and get product delivered will be through external partner sites.

Services that matter

"Joy of Age" makes it easy for its community members to find relevant and up-to-date service providers. Transparent information about source of the data and easy access to reviews from community members will assist elders and their caregivers towards making informed choices before engaging a service provider.
