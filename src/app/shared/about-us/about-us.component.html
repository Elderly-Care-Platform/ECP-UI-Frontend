About Us

The SEENAGE.LIFE online community has been designed and built with senior citizens in mind. Our community includes seniors and caregivers to the seniors -- they help decide what’s interesting and what is not for the community. Included in this core are the experts and a small group of evangelists who manage the website and ensure that the site is available to the community at all times.

What We Do

We are your exclusive trusted online community with answers to all your needs. Join us to interact, pursue interests, share articles, talk to experts, discover products, know about events happening around you.

Our Community

Join our online community to share your stories & have conversations based on your real life experiences. You can participate and drive the content of the conversations based on relevance or your interests.

Ask Experts

We partner with well-known experts across various functions relevant to the community. As members you can ask questions and receive answers in complete confidentiality from our experts.

Products that help

We present a curated set of useful products with every single best option available. Just pick what you want based on your need and get them delivered home, from reputed online markets. It’s that easy! You can also rate and review products on this platform after using them, to help others make better choices. Purchase and product delivery will be through reliable external partner sites.

Services that matter (currently for Hyderabad only)

We make it easy for our members to find relevant service providers. Transparent, updated information about the source of data and easy access to reviews from our community members will help you make informed choices before engaging a service provider.

Who We Are
