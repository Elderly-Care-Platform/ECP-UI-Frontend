What We Do

ABCD is the Elderly Care Platform supported by Tata Trust , a community-driven nationwide digital platform that powers an ecosystem to serve the needs of the elderly. ABCD is your trusted and non-prescriptive community to discuss issues , get advice , find relevant information, crowd-reviewed products, services and opportunities.

Our Community

ABCD is creating a digitally-driven online community that engages its members in real work - both locally and nationally. It will provide community-support to important interactions in a welcoming atmosphere .The key building block of the communities will be stories and conversations about the experiences collected over the life experiences of the elderly and their caregivers, resulting in a trusted warm space nurtured by the community members.

Servecies You Need

The platform makes it easy for its community members to find their service providers and all information regarding them in one place , which is relevant and up to date.
