The key objective of Seenage Life is of providing a platform for senior citizens to realize their potential and help them find a way back into the workforce. We believe that age does not limit ability, performance or skill and that seniors (seenagers) have a lot to contribute to society in terms of knowledge, experience, advice, guidance and also as role models. Our goal is to ensure that seenagers continue their professional involvement, have a sense of purpose and achievement while still earning.
